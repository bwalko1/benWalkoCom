Who is Ben Walko?

Hi! I am a Software Engineer that has a passion for working on enterprise level applications. I work for a company called MRI Software. My hobbies include video games and Rubik's cubes. I also attained the rank of Eagle Scout in 2012 when I was 16 years old.

What is MRI Software?

MRI Software has been around since 1971 and is a current leader in providing flexible software package that meets the unique needs of the real estate market. MRI has offices all over the world, including but not limited to United States, Canada, Australia, Hong Kong, Singapore, South Africa , and the United Kingdom.

Video games and Rubik's Cubes?

I have played many different video games, but some of the main ones include League of Legends, Minecraft, and Warframe. I have been playing them for as long as I can remember. I was always on the computer or gaming console as a child and I have never looked back since then.

Rubik's Cubes always fascinated me and I always admired people who could solve them, so I decided to get into them myself. Several years later my collection has grown to many different sizes, shapes and colors of unique Rubik's Cubes.
