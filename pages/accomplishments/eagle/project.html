Eagle Scout Project

The Eagle Scout Service Project, or simply Eagle Project, is the opportunity for a Boy Scout, Varsity Scout, or qualified Venturer in the Boy Scouts of America (BSA) to demonstrate leadership of others while performing a project for the benefit of his community.

My Eagle Scout project was to build three humane, foldable cat traps for my local cat rescue non-profit organization. The traps will help lower the population of local cats by spaying/neutering trapped cats, or giving cats forever homes by adopting them out.
